Take it anywhere

Put the hype on every phone on the team. One tap from the home screen, no app store needed.

iPhone & iPad

  1. Open this page in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the box with the up arrow).
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add and the megaphone icon lands on your home screen.

Android & Chrome

  1. Tap Add to phone below (or the menu ⋮).
  2. Choose Install app / Add to Home screen.
  3. Confirm, and it opens full screen like a real app.
